Tools and Technologies Used
• Python Libraries: Utilized pandas for data manipulation, matplotlib and seaborn for data visualization.

Key insights and findings.
• Race Winners: Identified top drivers with the most wins during the 2022 season.
• Driver Points: Analyzed and compared the total points scored by each driver.
